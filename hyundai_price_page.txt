Check Hyundai CRETA Car Price (Ex-showroom*)

The Hyundai CRETA is available with a starting price of ₹10,90,700* (ex-showroom) and is offered in multiple variants across petrol, diesel, and turbo petrol engine options. Buyers can choose from manual, IVT, automatic, and dual-clutch transmission configurations depending on the selected variant. The SUV lineup is structured to offer different combinations of performance, technology, and comfort features to meet a wide range of driving requirements.

The Hyundai CRETA is available in different variants such as E, EX, EX(O), S(O), SX, SX Premium, and several special editions. Each variant includes a different equipment package, allowing buyers to select features based on their daily usage and budget preferences. The petrol engine is suited to regular city and highway driving, while the diesel engine supports longer-distance travel with higher torque. Buyers looking for quicker acceleration and stronger highway response can explore the turbo petrol variants paired with the 7-speed DCT gearbox.

The SUV features a modern exterior design with several styling elements offered across the variant lineup. Depending on the selected variant, it is available with horizon LED positioning lamps, quad-beam LED headlamps, connected LED tail lamps, a parametric radiator grille, and alloy wheel options. Hyundai additionally offers dual-tone exterior colours and exclusive styling elements on selected trims and special editions.

The interior of the Hyundai CRETA includes multiple comfort and convenience features designed for everyday travel. Buyers can access a panoramic sunroof, ventilated front seats, wireless charging, dual-zone automatic climate control, rear-seat recline function, digital instrument cluster, and a connected infotainment system, depending on the selected variant. Higher trims also include Hyundai Bluelink connected car technology and a Bose premium sound system.

The Hyundai CRETA also includes several special editions with additional styling and equipment updates. The Knight Edition features a black exterior, matte black alloy wheels, brass-coloured cabin inserts, and exclusive Knight badging. The Summer Edition features selected convenience additions such as a dashcam, rear window sunshade, and upgraded display functions across specific trims. Buyers can additionally explore the King Edition, based on the Knight trim, with exclusive accessories and selected automatic petrol and diesel powertrain options.

Safety equipment across the Hyundai CRETA lineup includes six airbags, electronic stability control, hill-start assist control, tyre pressure monitoring system, and rear parking sensors. Higher variants additionally offer Hyundai SmartSense Level 2 ADAS features, including lane-keeping assist, forward collision avoidance assist, blind-spot collision warning, rear cross-traffic collision avoidance assist, and smart cruise control with stop-and-go functionality.


Discover the Hyundai CRETA Price in your State

The Hyundai CRETA price varies depending on the selected variant, engine option, transmission type, and feature package. Buyers can explore petrol, diesel, and turbo petrol configurations across E, EX, EX(O), S(O), SX, SX Premium, Knight Edition, Summer Edition, and King Edition variants. Manual, IVT, automatic, and DCT gearbox options are available across selected trims.

Across different states and cities, the Hyundai CRETA continues to suit a variety of driving conditions, ranging from daily city commuting to highway travel. Features such as panoramic sunroof, connected infotainment system, ventilated seats, Level 2 ADAS, and multiple drive modes allow buyers to choose a variant based on their driving preferences and everyday travel requirements.
